Understanding the mind for societal change


Lecture Series 2021

The ambition of this lecture series is to create a platform for ideas outside the common and settled "cognitive science culture” and induce a progressive discussion on how to research the mind. With the presented talks, we want to make a start and thereby provide an incentive to critically reflect upon the fundamental beliefs and approaches of cognitive science research in general. Today, we find that cognitive science research is dominated by very few disciplines, many perspectives are disregarded and receive little to no attention. As such, we want to integrate new, different and usually disregarded directions (such as climate change research, alternative epistemologies, critical AI, and critical race theory) within the research field of cognitive science.
